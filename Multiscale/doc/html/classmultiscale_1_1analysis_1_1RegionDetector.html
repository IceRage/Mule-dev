void RegionDetector::processImage ( vector< Region > &  regions)
private

Process the given image.

Apply filters to the image, threshold it, find its contours, approximate the polygons from these contours. Afterwards, process the polygons to find their distance from the origin, their area and the angle determined by the points from the contour which are on the edge and the closest point to the origin. Return all the polygons together with the processed information as a vector of regions.

Parameters
regionsThe regions from the image

double RegionDetector::regionAngle ( const vector< Point > &  polygon,
unsigned int  closestPointIndex 
)
private

Compute the angle of the polygon.

Compute the angle determined by the closest point to the origin and the points P1 and P2. These points are obtained from the intersection of the polygon with the line AB, determined by points A and B. Points A and B are the middle points of the sides of the rotated rectangle enclosing the polygon that are orthogonal to the line which is the nearest to the closestPoint.

closestPointIndexIndex of the closest point to the origin from the set of points defining the polygon
